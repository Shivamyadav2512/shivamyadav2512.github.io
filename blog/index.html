How does it feel to drop phone for a week: Unplugged

So i stopped using phone for a week. No calls. No messages. Nothing.

After randomly scrolling through the play store i stumbledupon an app called "Offtime" which let you avoid using your phone for a specific time. I used to think that i spend less time on phone but app's analytics shows something else. I had 100+ session on my phone daily and most used apps were WhatsApp, Instagram and Facebook. My device usage was 5 hours a day.

So i ended up doing self reflection and decided to stop using phone for a week. I switched off my phone and used my email address for the communication. It was great experience as i felt more calm and was getting more time to actually do things with less or no interruptions.

What i learned?

  • Sometimes its okay not to be connected with the world or getting overwhelmed with all the information.
  • You get more time for yourself
  • Less or no interruptions
  • You feel out of the loop

Result :
My phone usage is significantly less now. I don't check my phone's notification for more than 3 times in a day.
